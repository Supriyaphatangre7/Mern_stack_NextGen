Chicken feed is primarily composed of a mix of grains and protein sources, carefully formulated to meet the nutritional needs of poultry. The main ingredients typically include corn and soybean meal, which provide essential carbohydrates and proteins. These formulations are often developed by certified animal nutritionists to ensure a balanced diet for optimal growth and health of the chickens..

Nutritional Composition: Chicken feed is typically composed of grains (like corn and wheat), protein sources (such as soybean meal or fish meal), vitamins, and minerals to ensure balanced nutrition for growth, health, and egg production..

Supplementary Diet: Chickens can also benefit from foraging for insects, seeds, and greens, which provide variety and additional nutrients to their diet. This is especially common in free-range systems..
